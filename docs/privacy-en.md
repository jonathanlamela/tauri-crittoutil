# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 31, 2025

Thank you for choosing **Critto Util**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user data in our application.

## Collection and Use of Information

**Critto Util** does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The application operates entirely locally on the user's device without transmitting information to remote servers.

## Required Permissions

The app may request certain system permissions for its proper functioning. However, these permissions are not used to collect personal information.

## Third-Party Services

Since **Critto Util** does not collect data, it does not share information with third-party services.

## Data Security

As the app does not collect personal data, no specific security mechanisms are required to protect user information.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be informed of any changes by publishing the update in this section of the app or on our website.

## Contact

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: [info@jonathanlamela.it](mailto:info@jonathanlamela.it)
